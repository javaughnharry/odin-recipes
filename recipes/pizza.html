This pizza is unmatched. This pizza is powerful!

Ingredients

    Dough

  • 1 ½ cups (355ml) warm water (110deg F)
  • 1 package (2 ¼ tsp) active dry yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar or honey
  • 3 ½ – 4 cups (438–500g) all-purpose flour or bread flour
  • 2 tbsp (30ml) olive oil, plus 1 tbsp for bowl
  • 1 tsp salt

    Toppings

  • ½ cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Optional: Pepperoni, fresh basil, veggies

Steps

  1. Activate Yeast: Whisk warm water, sugar, and yeast in a bowl.
    Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until foamy.
  2. Make Dough: Add flour, salt, and olive oil. Knead by hand or with
    a mixer hook for 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  3. Rise: Lightly grease a large bowl, place dough inside, and cover.
    Let it rise in a warm spot for 60–90 minutes, or until doubled in size.
  4. Preheat: Preheat oven to 500°F (260°C). If using a pizza stone,
    place it in the oven while it heats.
  5. Shape: Punch down the dough and divide in half. On a floured surface,
    stretch or roll each half into a 12-inch circle.
  6. Add Toppings: Place dough on a baking sheet sprinkled with cornmeal.
    Spread sauce thinly, followed by cheese and toppings.
  7. Bake: Bake for 10–12 minutes, until the crust is golden brown and cheese
    is bubbling.
