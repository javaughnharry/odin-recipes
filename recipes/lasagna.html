Best Lasagna

This lasagna is the best of the best!
This lasagna is unmatched. This lasagna is powerful!

Ingredients

    Meat Sauce

  • 1 lb ground beef & 1 lb Italian sausage
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cans (28 oz each) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tbsp Italian seasoning & 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tbsp sugar (to balance acidity)
  • Salt & black pepper to taste

    Cheese Mixture & Pasta

  • 1 lb (16 oz) ricotta cheese
  • 1 lb mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1 large egg (to bind filling)
  • 1 package (16 oz) lasagna noodles

Steps

  1. Prep & Sauce: Cook ground beef and sausage in a large pot over medium-high heat.
    Add onions and garlic, cooking until softened. Stir in tomatoes, tomato paste, sugar,
    and herbs. Simmer for 30–60 minutes to thicken.
  2. Boil Noodles: Cook pasta in boiling, salted water for 8-10
    minutes (or until al dente). Drain and lay flat on foil to prevent sticking.
  3. Prepare Filling: In a bowl, mix ricotta, Parmesan, half the
    mozzarella, and the egg.
  4. Assemble Layers: Preheat oven to 375°F. Spread 1 cup of meat sauce on the bottom
    of a 9x13-inch pan. Top with:
    1. 3-4 Noodles
    2. 1/3 of ricotta mixture
    3. 1 cup meat sauce & mozzarella
    4. Repeat layers 2-3 times, ending with sauce and remaining mozzarella on top.
  5. Bake: Cover with foil (tip: spray with oil to prevent sticking) and bake for 30 minutes.
    Remove foil and bake for another 15 minutes until bubbly.
  6. Rest: Let the lasagna sit for 15-20 minutes before slicing to help it set.
