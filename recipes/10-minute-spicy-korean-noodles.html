10-minute noodles

Best 10-Minute Noodles

These 10-minute noodles are the best of the best!
These 10-minute noodles are unmatched. These 10-minute noodles are powerful!

Ingredients

  • 1 serving of noodles (ramen, udon, or somyeon)
  • 1.5 cups broth (chicken, beef, or vegetable)
  • The Sauce: 1 tbsp Gochujang, 2 tbsp oyster sauce,
    ½ tbsp soy sauce, ½ tbsp rice vinegar, 1 tsp sugar,
    and 1 minced garlic clove.

Steps

  1. Prep Noodles: Boil noodles according to package instructions, drain, and set aside.
  2. Make Flavor Base: In a bowl, combine the garlic, chopped green onions, and sugar.
    Sizzle them by pouring 1–2 tbsp of smoking hot oil over them.
  3. Mix Soup: Stir in the Gochujang, oyster sauce, soy sauce, and vinegar. Pour in hot
    broth and stir well.
  4. Assemble: Add the noodles to the spicy broth. Garnish with sesame seeds and green onions.
